Hotel Mount Paradise-Nagarkot

Hotel Mount Paradise is a budget-friendly, vegetarian, and vegan-friendly retreat located in the serene heart of Nagarkot, Nepal. Just 32 km from Tribhuvan International Airport, 20 km from the historic center of Bhaktapur, and 35 km from Thamel, it offers a peaceful escape from the chaotic city life. Surrounded by lush greeneries, where alarms are replaced by the gentle chirping of birds, it's an ideal spot for yoga, meditation, and digital detox. The hotel is close to the local market, bus stop, and popular hiking trails, making it perfect for long staycations with self-cooking options.


Guests can explore a variety of nearby attractions that showcase the beauty and culture of Nagarkot. Visit the tranquil Buddha Peace Park, cross the scenic Rani Jhula, or experience the thrill of paragliding and ziplining. For nature lovers, hiking trails abound, offering stunning views of sunrise and sunset. Immerse yourself in the charm of local village life, where you can connect with the community and experience the peaceful simplicity of rural Nepal.
